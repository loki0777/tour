ABOUT:

I am NYOMAN ZENG. You can call me NYOMAN.

Did you know, in Balinese, NYOMAN means second child?

I have been a tour guide and tourist driver for more than 8 years. I enjoy meeting people from around the world and getting to know their different cultures. I believe that the best way my guests can experience Bali is by introducing them to our culture and tradition. To make my guests feel safe, I always take the time to explain the hotels and tourist spots. I can offer a comfortable car ride with music from your home and stories for my home.


It is my pleasure to make you feel at home in Bali. You don't have to worry about me waiting - my hours for you are flexible and friendly. If you need a photographer, I can do that for you, too.

This has been a passion even with my 2 years experience in a cruise ship and 10 years experience in the hotel industry. I look forward to showing you the beauty that is my home, Bali.
